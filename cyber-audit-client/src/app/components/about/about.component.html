Cyber Security Risk Assesment

The Cybersecurity Education, Research, and Outreach Center (CEROC) at Tennessee Tech provides cybersecurity risk assessment services for regional and statewide, small to medium-sized manufacturing companies.
Through this program, CEROC seeks to:

  • Provide a cybersecurity risk assessment for stakeholders of small to middle-sized manufacturers identifying opportunities for improvement of their cybersecurity position.
  • Help organizations understand their security posture based upon client request.
  • Provide recommendations to aid in improvement of organizational security posture.
  • Increase overall regional and statewide awareness of cybersecurity issues addressable through common-sense solutions.
  • Cybersecurity training techniques and solutions using feedback from industry.

The Cybersecurity Risk Assessment is based upon the NIST Cybersecurity Framework via the key areas of Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover. Our assessment uses a summarized set of these areas customizing the experience for the specific needs of small to medium-sized businesses.

Assessment convenience and flexibility is an important component of our work assuring that we provide our client with a quality experience, which is respectful of challenging schedules. This is accomplished by focusing on areas of most concern to our clients while utilizing online data collection to conserve client time investment. Our risk assessment teams are a unique mix of undergraduate and graduate cybersecurity students with a passion for the field led by an information technology/cybersecurity professional.
