Cyber Security Risk Assesment

Welcome!

The Cybersecurity Education, Research, and Outreach Center (CEROC) at Tennessee Tech provides cybersecurity risk assessment services for regional and statewide, small to medium-sized manufacturing companies. Through this program, CEROC seeks to:

  • Provide a cybersecurity risk assessment for stakeholders
  • Help organizations understand their security posture
  • Provide recommendations to aid in improvement
  • Increase overall regional and statewide awareness of cybersecurity issues
  • Improve cybersecurity training techniques and solutions

This is a FREE tool for businesses in the state of Tennessee, with the purpose of helping increase cybersecurity awareness across the state. To use this tool, start by registering below to make an account. After this, you will be able to take a survey that will ask you questions about your organization’s cybersecurity and information technology habits and policies. After submitting your survey, one of CEROC’s risk assessment teams will take a look and send you a report with recommendations of where and how you could improve your organization’s cybersecurity, organized by severity.
